WHERE:

    - =, IS...
        Check for equality
        "=" for most cases
        "IS..." for comparison to NULL (and others)
    - <>, !=, IS NOT...
        Check for inequality
        "!=" is an alias dfor "<>"
        "IS NOT..." for comparition to NULL (and others)
    - > / >=                                             |
        Check for greater than / greater or equal        | BEATWEEN
    - < / <=                                             |
        Check for smaller than / smaller or equal        |
    - AND, OR
